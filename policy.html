Return Policy

At Ajay Enterprises, we do not have a general return policy for the products we sell. However, in the event that a product is damaged, a return policy will apply.

Before purchasing, we ensure that customers are shown samples and given a clear understanding of the product. If, after purchasing, a customer wishes to return the product, returns will not be accepted unless the product is damaged.

We trust and hope that our customers will support us in maintaining this policy and understand the importance of the process.

Factory Timings

Monday to Saturday: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM

Leave Policy

  • Two Leave Days per Month: Every employee is entitled to two leave days per month. Employees must inform their manager at least three days in advance.
  • Emergency Leave: No advance notice needed. Emergency leave will be granted without issues.
  • Public Holidays: All public holidays or festivals will be treated as general holidays.
